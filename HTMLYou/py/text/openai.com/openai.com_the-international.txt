About Dota 2

 Dota 2 is a complex, team-based game played by over ten million people monthly. It has a thriving pro scene with over $20M awarded in The International alone.
The game is continuous, has hidden information, and good strategy and positioning win over raw mechanics. We use Dota 2 as a testbed for new AI technologies.









Built by OpenAI, in collaboration with Valve. Our infrastructure partner is Microsoft Azure.
